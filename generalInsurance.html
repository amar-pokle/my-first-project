    How Insurance Renewals Work?


    An insurance policy benefits you only until the time it does not expire. You need to ensure that your policy does not lapse. This ensures the continuity of
    coverage and protects you against unforeseen risks. In addition, the No Claim Bonus (NCB) also gets carried forward and does not lapse if you make no claim
    during a policy year. Here is how you can renew your insurance policy easily online and on time:

    • Keep a tab on your renewal date
    • To begin, visit ICICI Bank website
    • Click on the Insurance tab
    • Follow the instructions to complete the process
    • Make the payment through Internet Banking, Credit or Debit Card
    • Acknowledge the payment receipt along with reference number

    How Claims Work?


    Reimbursement Claims:


    The benefit of an insurance policy can be availed by filing an insurance claim. Here’s how you can initiate the process of filing a reimbursement insurance claim:

    • Contact the insurance helpline number
    • Provide relevant information about your policy
    • Allow the insurer to authenticate the claim request
    • Upon validation, your insurer’s representative will contact you
    • Submit all requisite documents to the insurer’s representative for his/ her report
    • The claims department will then process the claim and disburse the claim amount.

    Cashless Claims:


    Cashless claims are only available in network hospitals/ garages. Here are the steps needed to file a cashless claim:

    • Call the insurance helpline number to find out the nearest network facility
    • Display your insurance card at the facility to avail cashless insurance
    • Fill the cashless claim request form and provide details of the insurer
    • Send a fax of the claim form and relevant documents to the insurer
    • The insurer will then review the claim and either accept or reject it
    • If approved, the bill will be directly paid to the establishment by the insurer.
