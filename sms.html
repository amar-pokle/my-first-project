    Features

    Available to customers registered for SMS Alert.

    Track your accounts, round the clock.

    according to services) through SMS to 5607040.

    Be in smart position to detect any unauthorised access to your accounts.

    SMS "PNB PROD" to 5607040 for list of services offered.






    Registration for signature SMS Banking

    Visit any nearest Signature branch and get your Mobile Number registered in the account.

    Usage tips for customers- Banking operations

    Check account balance, get Mini statement, get status of cheques, Execute stop payment of cheques and self
    transfer of funds (Rs. 5000/- daily limit) anytime.

    To avail, send an SMS with the specified keyword to the SMS Banking number 5607040

    For Balance Inquiry

    BAL /space/ 16 digit Account Number

    e.g: BAL 015300XXXXXXXXXX

    For Mini Statement Inquiry

    MINSTMT /space/ 16 digit Account Number
    e.g. MINSTMT 015300XXXXXXXXXX

    For Self Transfer of funds (Daily limit Rs. 5,000/- ):

    SLFTRF /space/ FROM A/C /space/ TO A/C /space/ AMOUNT
    e.g. SLFTRF 015300YYYYYYYYYY 015300XXXXXXXXXX 100

    For Cheque Status Inquiry:

    CHQINQ /space/ CHEQUE NUMBER /space/ 16 digit Account Number
    e.g. CHQINQ 123456 015300XXXXXXXXXX

    For Stop Payment of Cheque:

    STPCHQ /space/ CHEQUE NO. /space/ 16 digit Account number
    e.g. STPCHQ 123456 015300XXXXXXXXXX
