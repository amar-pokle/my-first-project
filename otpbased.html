    OTP Based Aadhaar Seeding



    OTP based aadhaar seeding and authentication of accounts

    In terms of amendments made under Prevention of Money Laundering (Maintenance of Records) Second Amendment Rules, 2017, all accounts need to be
    aadhaar seeded and authenticated with biometric or Yes-No facility.

    In order to facilitate the customers to seed aadhaar in accounts/authenticate already seeded accounts, our bank has come out with a secure web page
    through which any customer can do the same sitting at home. Since the seeding/authentication is OTP based, only those customers whose mobile numbers
    are updated in bank accounts as well as in aadhaar database can use this facility.



    For Aadhaar Based Seeding


    We suggest the following:

    ❶ Step1:
    Please Enter the valid account number and click on the "Continue" button , Then you will get OTP (One Time Password ) to your registered Mobile.

    ❷ Step2:
    Enter the OTP & Captcha and click on Validate button , Once the OTP is validated your will be redirected to another page which prompt for Aadhaar
    Number.

    ❸ Step3:
    Enter the Aadhaar Number click on "continue" Button, then you will get OTP on registerd mobile with UAIDI. Enter the received OTP from UAIDI
    and validate it by clicking on "Validate " Button . Once it is successfull you will be shown your personal details.
